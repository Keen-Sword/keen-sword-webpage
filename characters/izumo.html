Izumo

Izumo is an Ajisain shipgirl and one of the main protagonists in the story of Keen Sword. She has a distinct appearance with her orange and white fur, large fluffy tail and exposed fang on her right side. This makes her look similar to her sister Kaga.

Her eyes range from dark blue to a lighter blue, while her hair is a dark gray and relatively long where it ends naturally. The fox ears she has have a lighter shade than her other hair and have a moderate amount of fluff in them. She also has a pair of dark brown paws.

Usually, she wears a gray hoodie together with a white-red serafuku, a long red plated skirt, and a pair of white knee-high socks. The zipper line on her hoodie is highlighted in yellow with a pair of dashed white lines on either side, and her serafuku has an anchor printed on it. However, she has also been depicted wearing a pink kimono on occasion.

Her personality is very open and adaptable, as she is always eager to learn new things and interact with new people. It is also somewhat playful, but she is also a gap moe.
