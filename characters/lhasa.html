Lhasa

Lhasa is an Chugonese shipgirl and a minor character in the story of Keen Sword. She has medium-length, straight hair colored in a muted purple-brown tone, with one strain of her falling over her shoulder. Her eyes are brown to dark brown but are usually hidden.

Normally, she wears her long brown coat open with a beige shirt underneath. The coat is long enough to cover her green pleated skirt, showing only her dark pantyhose. She also has a stylized golden hair clip shaped as a phoenix that she is not allowed to wear, but does so anyway. Her coat also has a hood that makes her significantly harder to detect when used, making her face disappear and only showing glowing white eyes.

She is largely apathetic to the world around her and does not particularly care about others. However, she is also nationalistic and epically likes to annoy Haguro by standing right outside the ajisain border.
