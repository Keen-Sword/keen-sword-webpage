Ajisai

Ajisai, officially The State of Ajisai, is an island nation and parliamentary constitutional monarchy located in the Pacific Ocean, near the coast of Not-China and the Orient mainland. It consists of five major islands in the Ajisain archipelago, where it borders the Occiental Federation, along with numerous smaller islands scattered across the Pacific.

Ajisai is heavily industrialized and densely populated. It is one of the three nations that maintain a large standing fleet and active naval presence, a reflection of its economic and strategic importance. For the same reason, it plays a key role in the story of Keen Sword.

Internationally, Ajisai maintains cordial relations with the Continental Union, which serves as its de jure security and defense guarantor due to its official demilitarized status. Nevertheless, its powerful fleet remains a persistent source of tension with Not-China.
